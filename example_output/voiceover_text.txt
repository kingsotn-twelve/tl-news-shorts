A3, Wörth an der Donau, Germany

In Wörth an der Donau, Germany, a major accident on the A3 involved three trucks, leading to the highway's closure. A truck carrying hazardous materials overturned, causing a spill. The police secured the area, ensuring there was no immediate danger, as rescue teams extracted a trapped driver, now hospitalized. Hazmat teams stayed on scene, handling the situation until midday.
